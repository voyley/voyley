Frontend

We are looking for individuals with a keen eye in design for the position of Front End Developer. Front end Developers are responsible for ensuring the alignment of web design and user experience requirements, and maintaining brand consistency across all web pages, among other duties. Front End Developers are required to work in teams alongside Back end Developers and Graphic Designers to ensure all elements of web creation are consistent. This requires excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Responsibilities:
  • Assisting graphic designer with website/app design
  • Building and maintaining website according to design
  • Develop features to enhance the user experience
  • Maintain brand consistency throughout design
Requirements:
  • Proficiency with HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • Experience with responsive design
  • Experience working with RESTFUL API
  • Experience with React Native
Preferred Skills:
  • Experience with iOS and/or Android
  • Experience with payment solutions
  • Experience with SOAP protocol
  • Full stack web developer

Backend

We are looking for an analytical, results-driven Back-end Developer who will work with team members to troubleshoot and improve back-end applications and processes.

Responsibilities:
  • Collaborate with front end developers to design functional and cohesive code to enhance customer experience
  • Develop APIs for internal usage
  • Build and manage internal databases
Requirements:
  • Proficiency with JavaScript and/or Python
  • Experience with relational database and/or NoSQL database
  • Experience developing APIs
  • Knowledge of data structures and algorithms
Preferred Skills:
  • Experience with SOAP protocol
  • Experience with route finding algorithms
  • Experience with deep learning algorithms
  • Full stack web developer

Content Creator

The content writer will be a key member of our fast-growing and high-performing team, writing and editing short-form and long-form content pieces that build brand awareness, drive organic traffic, and engage our potential customers. Current marketing channels would be our upcoming blog and social media platforms. We want our travellers to no longer treat trains and buses as the second choice of travel. Help us to spread the word of eco-travelling!

Requirements:
  • Edit, proofread, and review content submissions, ensuring proper usage of brand voice and style, grammar, message clarity, and alignment with the marketing and content strategy
  • Conduct research and utilize data to inform copy creation and identify new content opportunities that will engage customers
  • Understanding of basic SEO principles and best practices, with the ability to stay on top of changing search trends and Google algorithm updates
  • Strong time management skills and ability to work on multiple projects
  • Eager to learn and implement new tools and methodologies
  • Experience working for a startup is a plus
