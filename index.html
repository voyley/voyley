Who We Are

Voyley is an international team based in Stockholm that aim to encourage travellers to choose more eco-friendly travel alternatives such as buses and trains. We make planning and booking tickets via a multi-modal transport service more flexible and provide customised options for travellers to plan out their adventures!

What We Do

Voyley is a web app/smartphone app that gives travellers the flexibility to plan and book tickets hassle free while on the go. Using our 'mix and match' ticketing system, you can now customise and curate your journey to cater to your overall travel needs!
